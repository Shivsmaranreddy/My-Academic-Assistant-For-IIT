Prerequisite Information

To effectively solve the problems in this module, a solid understanding of the following foundational concepts is essential:

  • Basic Trigonometric Ratios: Definition of sine, cosine, tangent, cotangent, secant, and cosecant in a right-angled triangle.
  • Trigonometric Ratios for Standard Angles: Values of trigonometric functions for $0^\circ, 30^\circ, 45^\circ, 60^\circ, 90^\circ$.
  • Quadrant System (ASTC Rule): Signs of trigonometric functions in the four quadrants and the concept of allied angles (e.g., $90^\circ \pm \theta$, $180^\circ \pm \theta$).
  • Fundamental Trigonometric Identities:
    • $\sin^2\theta + \cos^2\theta = 1$
    • $1 + \tan^2\theta = \sec^2\theta$
    • $1 + \cot^2\theta = \csc^2\theta$
  • Algebraic Manipulation: Proficiency in simplifying expressions, solving equations, and using techniques like componendo and dividendo.
  • Triangle Properties: Basic properties of triangles, such as the sum of angles being $180^\circ$ ($\pi$ radians).
